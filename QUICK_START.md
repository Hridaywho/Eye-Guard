## ğŸ¥ When to Take Breaks

### Warning Signs
- Blink rate drops below 10/min
- Eyes feel dry or irritated
- Vision becomes blurry
- Headache or eye strain
- Red or watery eyes

### Recommended Actions
1. **Stop** and close eyes for 30 seconds
2. **Look away** from screen at distant objects
3. **Blink exercises**: 10 complete blinks slowly
4. **20-20-20 rule**: Every 20 min, look 20 feet away for 20 sec
5. **Take a break**: Walk around for 5-10 minutes
